# Plan for Real Estate Landing Page

## Design Goals
- **Simplicity:** Short paragraphs, bold benefits, no jargon.
- **Visuals:** 12 strategic image placeholders to break up text and show "proof" of tools.
- **Conversion:** Focus on "saving time" and "closing deals".

## Image Placement Strategy
1.  **Hero/Header:** A welcoming, aspirational image of a modern home + "For Sale" sign to immediately contextualize the service.
2.  **Introduction:** An image of a productive agent (dashboard view) to visually represent the "admin time gone" benefit.
3.  **Basic Level:**
    -   *Claude AI Chat Interface:* Showing a draft email/summary to prove simplicity.
    -   *Calendar/CRM Dashboard:* Showing organized appointments.
4.  **Intermediate Level:**
    -   *Canva Magic Studio:* A "Just Listed" graphic being generated.
    -   *Social Media Carousel:* Example of the output on a phone screen.
    -   *Video Editing Tool:* Simple timeline view (Opus/CapCut).
5.  **Advanced Level:**
    -   *Automated Workflow Diagram:* Simple visual of "Lead -> Email -> Appointment".
    -   *Performance Analytics:* Charts showing lead engagement/ROI.
    -   *Google Antigravity/Agent Interface:* A "smart agent" dashboard.
6.  **Example Section:**
    -   *Input:* Photo of the raw property (kitchen/exterior).
    -   *Output 1:* The polished Zillow description (text snippet).
    -   *Output 2:* The HeyGen Avatar Video thumbnail.
    -   *Output 3:* The Instagram Carousel graphic.
